Quantitative Aptitude — Subtopics

  • Percentages Fraction conversions, successive changes, population models
  • Profit & Loss Cost/marked/selling prices, margins, successive discounts
  • Ratio & Proportion Directly/inversely proportional terms, partnerships, age problems
  • Mixtures & Alligation Mean prices, weighted averages, liquid solutions
  • Interest Simple & compound interest, annual/half-yearly rates, installments
  • Time & Work Individual efficiencies, combined work, pipes and cisterns
  • Speed & Distance Relative speed, train problems, boats and streams
  • Averages Basic averages, group changes, weighted averages
  • Properties Prime, composite, integers, rational & irrational numbers
  • Divisibility Rules, cyclicity, unit digits, remainder theorems
  • HCF & LCM Factors, multiples, product relationships
  • Simplification BODMAS, fractions, decimals, surds, indices
  • Equations Linear & quadratic equations, roots properties
  • Progressions Arithmetic (AP), Geometric (GP), Harmonic (HP) series
  • Permutations & Combinations Factorials, arrangements, selections, circular seating
  • Probability Event outcomes, coins, dice, playing cards, bags
  • Logarithms Laws, base conversions, characteristic/mantissa
  • Lines & Angles Parallel lines, transversals, angle bisectors
  • Triangles Congruency, similarity, area, centroid, incenter
  • Circles Tangents, secants, chords, arc length theorems
  • 2D Mensuration Rectangles, squares, polygons — areas & perimeters
  • 3D Mensuration Cubes, cylinders, cones, spheres — volumes & surface areas
  • Coordinate Geometry Distance, midpoint, slopes, intercept forms
  • Tables Raw numerical grids, missing values, percentage growth
  • Graphs Bar graphs, line charts, pie charts, radar charts
  • Caselets Paragraph-based data, Venn diagrams, tabular sorting
  • Data Sufficiency Evaluating if statements can solve a question

General Intelligence & Reasoning — Subtopics

  • Analogy Semantic, symbolic & number-based relationships
  • Classification Odd-word out, odd-pair out, letter groups
  • Coding-Decoding Letter shifting, numerical coding, deciphering messages
  • Blood Relations Family trees, coded relations, pointing-to photographs
  • Direction & Distance Cardinal directions, angular turns, shortest distance
  • Order & Ranking Position from ends, interchanging ranks, total count
  • Syllogism Venn diagram analysis, statements & logical conclusions
  • Mathematical Operations Symbol substitutions, balancing equations, BODMAS
  • Seating Arrangement Linear rows, circular shapes, square tables
  • Puzzles Scheduling, floor-based ordering, matrices, color/attribute matching
  • Inequalities Coded inequalities, direct expressions, relationship conclusions
  • Input-Output Step-by-step rearrangement patterns for numbers & words
  • Clocks & Calendars Angle between hands, faulty clocks, leap years, day finding
  • Series Pattern continuation in a sequence of figures
  • Analogy & Classification Structural similarities or odd-one-out shapes
  • Mirror & Water Images Horizontal and vertical reflections
  • Paper Folding & Cutting Final shape after unfolding punched papers
  • Embedded Figures Detecting hidden target shapes in complex diagrams
  • Figure Completion Identifying the missing quadrant
  • Cubes & Dice Folded net variations, opposite faces, painted cube cuts
  • Statement & Assumptions Identifying unstated premises behind a claim
  • Statement & Conclusions Deriving direct logical consequences
  • Course of Action Finding practical solutions to a stated problem
  • Cause & Effect Identifying independent events, common causes, direct effects
  • Assertion & Reason Validating factual statements and their causal link

English Language & Comprehension — Subtopics

  • Main Idea & Title Central theme or best title for a passage
  • Inference-Based Conclusions not explicitly stated
  • Fact-Based Direct information, data, explicit details
  • Vocabulary in Context Meanings of words/phrases as used in text
  • Tone & Style Author's attitude — sarcastic, objective, critical
  • Parts of Speech Nouns, pronouns, adjectives, verbs, adverbs, prepositions, conjunctions
  • Subject-Verb Agreement Singular/plural subjects with correct verb forms
  • Tenses & Conditionals Correct time frames & hypothetical structures
  • Voice & Narration Active/passive voice, direct/indirect speech
  • Error Spotting Identifying grammatical mistakes
  • Sentence Correction Replacing underlined phrases correctly
  • Synonyms & Antonyms Words with similar or opposite meanings
  • Idioms & Phrases Idiomatic expressions & contextual meanings
  • One-Word Substitution Replacing lengthy phrases with a single word
  • Spelling Errors Detecting correct/incorrect spellings
  • Phrasal Verbs Verb-preposition combinations & applications
  • Para Jumbles Rearranging scrambled sentences coherently
  • Cloze Test Filling multiple blanks in a passage
  • Fill in the Blanks Single/double blanks testing grammar or vocabulary
  • Sentence Improvement Enhancing structure, clarity & conciseness

Tier-2 Paper 2: Statistical Investigator Grade II & JSO

Specialized paper for Statistical Investigator & Junior Statistical Officer posts.

  • Probability Classical, relative frequency, axiomatic definitions; addition & multiplication theorems; conditional probability; Bayes' theorem
  • Random Variables Discrete & continuous; pmf, pdf, cumulative distribution functions
  • Mathematical Expectation Expectations, moments, mgf, product moments, conditional expectations
  • Distributions Binomial, Poisson, Normal, Exponential, Geometric, Uniform — properties & applications
  • Data Types Primary & secondary data; classification & tabulation; questionnaire design
  • Diagrammatic Representation Bar diagrams, pie charts, histograms, frequency polygons, ogives, stem-and-leaf plots
  • Central Tendency Mean, median, mode, geometric mean, harmonic mean
  • Dispersion Range, quartile deviation, mean deviation, standard deviation, CV, moments
  • Skewness & Kurtosis Karl Pearson's & Bowley's coefficients; kurtosis based on moments
  • Bivariate Analysis Scatter diagrams; Pearson's correlation; Spearman's rank correlation
  • Linear Regression Simple linear regression; least squares; regression coefficients & properties
  • Sampling Concepts Population, sample, parameter, statistic, sampling frame, errors
  • Sampling Techniques SRS (with/without replacement), Stratified, Systematic, Cluster Sampling
  • Estimation Theory Point estimation, unbiasedness, consistency, efficiency, sufficiency, MLE basics
  • Interval Estimation Confidence intervals for mean, variance & proportions
  • Hypothesis Testing Null & alternative hypotheses, Type I & II errors, critical region, power of a test
  • Statistical Tests Z-test, t-test, F-test, Chi-Square test for goodness of fit/independence
  • ANOVA Gauss-Markov linear models; One-way & Two-way classification
  • Time Series Analysis Components (secular trend, seasonal, cyclical, irregular); moving average & least-squares trend
  • Index Numbers Laspeyre's, Paasche's, Fisher's ideal; Time Reversal & Factor Reversal tests; cost of living index
