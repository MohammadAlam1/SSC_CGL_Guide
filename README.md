# 📚 SSC Exam Preparation Hub

A comprehensive, single‑page web resource for SSC (Staff Selection Commission) exam aspirants.  
It brings together the complete Tier‑1 and Tier‑2 syllabus, recommended books, YouTube channels, and detailed subtopics in a clean, interactive, and mobile‑friendly interface.

## ✨ Features

- **Complete Syllabus Coverage** – Tier‑1 & Tier‑2 (Paper 1 & 2) with subject‑wise subtopics.
- **Resource Recommendations** – Books (R.S. Aggarwal, Lucent, SP Bakshi, etc.) and YouTube channels.
- **Interactive Navigation** – Smooth scrolling, sticky navbar, and accordion subtopics.
- **Elegant UI/UX** – Modern glass‑morphism design, soft shadows, gradient hero, and subtle animations.
- **Color‑Coded Sections** – Distinct accent colors for Quant, Reasoning, English, GK, and Statistics.
- **Tier‑2 Exam Pattern Table** – Detailed marks, negative marking, and qualifying modules.
- **Fully Responsive** – Adapts seamlessly to desktops, tablets, and smartphones.
- **No Dependencies** – Pure HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript; runs directly in any browser.

## 🧠 What’s Inside

- **Tier‑1 Sections**  
  - Quantitative Aptitude (Arithmetic, Number Systems, Algebra, Geometry, Data Interpretation)  
  - General Intelligence & Reasoning (Verbal, Logical, Non‑Verbal, Critical)  
  - English Language & Comprehension (Grammar, Vocabulary, Reading, Verbal Ability)  
  - General Awareness (Static GK, Current Affairs, Miscellaneous)

- **Tier‑2 Paper 1**  
  - Mathematical Abilities (Module 1)  
  - Reasoning & General Intelligence (Module 2)  
  - English Language & Comprehension (Module 1, Section 2)  
  - General Awareness (Module 2, Section 2)  
  - Computer Knowledge (Qualifying)  
  - Data Entry Speed Test (DEST – Qualifying)

- **Tier‑2 Paper 2 (Statistics)**  
  - Probability, Random Variables & Distributions  
  - Statistical Methods  
  - Correlation, Regression & Sampling  
  - Statistical Inference & Hypothesis Testing  
  - ANOVA, Time Series & Index Numbers

- **Book & Channel Library**  
  - Recommended “only one” book per subject.  
  - Top YouTube educators for each section.
